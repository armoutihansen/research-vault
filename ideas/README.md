# Ideas (Layer 2 → 3 bridge)

Cross-topic **candidate-idea harvests** — ranked, adversarially-vetted snapshots of research-project
ideas synthesized from the topic/area notes (`/topic-cluster` output). Dated, because they are
re-runnable as the corpus grows. The candidate ideas themselves also live inline in each topic note's
*Candidate ideas* section; `/promote-idea` turns a chosen one into `projects/<slug>.md` at
`status: feasibility`, back-linked to its source topic.
